Privacy Policy for Harmony Hub

Last updated on 26/01/2024

Welcome to Harmony Hub! This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with our privacy practices.

Types of Data Collected

We gather personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and address, which you freely supply when you interact with our website, sign up for emails, or attend events and activities. In addition, we may collect non-personal information such as browser type, device characteristics, and usage habits. This non-personal data is automatically gathered using cookies and other tracking technologies to improve your overall experience on our website. These metrics enable us to analyse use trends, enhance our services, and provide you a more personalised and optimised encounter.

Data Usage

We use the personal information you submit for a variety of purposes, such as communicating about Harmony Hub events, activities, and updates. This information is also used to complete registrations for events and activities, distribute newsletters and promotional materials with your permission, and reply to queries while providing customer service. Non-personal information, on the other hand, is used to analyse website usage trends, which ultimately helps us improve our services and user experiences. This data analysis provides us with insights into how users browse our site, allowing us to make educated decisions about how to optimise and modify our content for a more engaging and user-friendly experience.

User Rights

You have the right to view and rectify your personal information. If you would like to review or change the information we have on file, please contact us at [mvmg123@harmonyhub.com]. Furthermore, we respect your preferences by giving you the opportunity to opt-out of receiving promotional materials and newsletters. You can make this option by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us directly. Furthermore, you may request that we delete your personal information from our records. However, even after your personal data has been deleted, certain information may be preserved for legal or administrative purposes.

Security Measures

We prioritize the security of your information and have implemented appropriate measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any modifications will be effective immediately upon posting on this page.
